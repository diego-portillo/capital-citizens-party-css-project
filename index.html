about us

We care about your rights

We are a civil organization interested in social, economical, ecological and cultural change with over twenty years of activism for a better life in Asuncion city, Paraguay. We're allways open to recieve new allies to our cause.

The objectives of out democratic agenda includes: free accesible health and education, more rights and justices for students, workers, peasants, natives, women, LGTBIQ, and migrants, enviromental actions and more...

Our goals

Social justice

Ensure that individuals fulfill their societal roles and receive what was their due from society without differences of gender, origin, culture, religion or class.

Environmental Care

Work towards environmental protection and sustainability by striving to increase awareness of the efficient use of natural resources, by supporting eco-friendly activities and products.

Urban Planning

Organize efficient transport, health, security, entertainement and living services. Also better job oportunities and wealth distribution.
